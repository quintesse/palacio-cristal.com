This section contains all the programs I ever wrote that I found interesting enough to include on my site. All the programs here are either finished or at least out of beta, other projects I'm working on that are not finished yet or still in a conceptual stage can be found in the Current Projects section.

The following programs can be found here:

Easy Accent

A small program that enables you to easily enter international characters and special symbols not found on your keyboard.

DeeEnEs

Is just one of the programs that are written by people who have an account at one of the many sites that offer dynamic DNS services, like the one I use: www.dyndns.org and who need a way to have their IP addresses automatically updated. There are quite a few of these programs out there, I just didn't like any of them. That's the only reason for DeeEnEs' existence, not because I think it's brilliant, but I just didn't like the "competition".
