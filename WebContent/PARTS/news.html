Saturday 11, March 2000
Well this is it. I finally decided to publish my new site. As we can see if we look at this page I started designing this site quite some time ago. More than 2 years actually! And then I just started doing other things and I just let this new design collect dust in a manner of speaking. But since I bought myself a digital camera (a Nikon Coolpix 950) in November last year I have steadily been putting more and more pictures on the website, stashed in some corner with no link to them. So this called for another decision to be made: would I or would I not allow the whole world to see all the pictures I've made? Well the result you can see right here on the "My Photos" page.

So is the site finished? Well, of course not. First rule of a successful website is that it is never finished. But am I close to finished? Well.... uhhh... not really. There's still the introduction on the main page that is still non-existent and the same goes for the "My Interests" section which is empty.
